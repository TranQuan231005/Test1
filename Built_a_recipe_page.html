SPAGHETTI BOLOGNESS

Please remember that the Australia tablespoons is 20ml, wheareas the American tablespoon is only 14ml. So you need to convert it accordingly to wherever you live

INGRETDIENTS

    1kg of learn mince meat

    1 medium onion (diced)

    1 tablespoon of Masterfoods Spaghetti Bolognese herbs blend

    1 tin of diced tomatoes

    1 jar of Leggos Bolognese sauce (or any brand will do)

    1.5 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce

    1 tablespoon of borwn sugar

    1-1 ½ teaspoon of salt

METHOD:

  • Heat the saucepan and spray a small amount of oil in pan.
  • Add 1 tablespoon of Masterfoods spaghetti Bolognese herbs blend and stir Bolognese sauce and stir through
  • Add 1.5 tablespoond off Worchestshire sauce and approxiamtely 2 tablespoonds of sweet chilli sauce and stir through
  • Then add 1 tablespoon of bown sugar. To start off with add 1 teaspoon of salt, stir through and taste. If more sugar is required a futher ½ teaspoon can be added. Let simmer on a low heat for about 15 mins.
  • Serve with any pasta or Spaghetti. Enjoy
